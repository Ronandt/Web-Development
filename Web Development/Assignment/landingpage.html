A look into to my mechanical keyboard interest

How I got into keyboards

I got into mechanical keyboards first, through buying a cheap 'gaming' mechanical keyboard off a store. Ever since then, I have been intrigued with the inner workings of mechanical keyboards and through self-discovery.

Why Keyboards?

Use

Some might say that keyboards are unimportant because they're just a tool that drives what we think on the computer. However, I think it is because of that reason why keyboards are so important. Why not get something that you enjoy typing on often?

Aesthetics

In current times, the aesthetics of a keyboard one would imagine is a black keyboard with RGB lightning. Why not customise your keyboard to something you exactly want, instead of the same old 'gaming' keyboards that are widely available on the market.

Motivation

A good custom mechanical keyboard, might just motivate you to do tasks or work. Just like having a good environment, even somethihng taken for granted like a keyboard will increase motivation and happiness.
